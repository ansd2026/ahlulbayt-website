Welcome to Our Community

A Home for Every
Believer

Ahlul Bayt Islamic Center of Columbus, Ohio, is a welcoming spiritual home for Muslims of all backgrounds. Rooted in the teachings of the Prophet Muhammad (PBUH) and his blessed household, we serve the Columbus community through worship, education, and service.

Whether you are visiting for the first time or returning to renew your connection with faith, you will find a warm community here. Our doors are open to all who seek knowledge, community, and spiritual growth.

What We Offer

Programs & Services

🕌
Daily Prayers

Five daily prayers led by our Imam. Jumu'ah (Friday) prayer is held every week with a khutbah in English and Arabic.

📖
Quran Classes

Weekly Quran recitation and Tajweed classes for all levels — children, youth, and adults — in a welcoming learning environment.

🎓
Islamic School

Sunday school programming for children covering Islamic studies, Arabic language, and Ahl al-Bayt history and values.

🤝
Community Services

Marriage services, funeral arrangements, counseling referrals, and support for new Muslims and converts.

🌙
Ramadan Programs

Nightly Iftar gatherings, Tarawih prayers, Laylat al-Qadr programs, and Eid celebrations for the whole family.

🫂
Youth & Sisters

Dedicated halaqa circles, events, and programs for youth groups and sisters, building strong community bonds.
